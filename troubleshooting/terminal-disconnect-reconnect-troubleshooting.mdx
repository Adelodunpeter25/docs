---
title: Disconnect and Reconnect Troubleshooting
description: Fix reconnect notifications and reconnect action issues.
---

## No disconnect notification appears

- Confirm the disconnect was not user-initiated
- Confirm session lifecycle listener is active (not removed by another service handler)
- Confirm tab/session mapping exists (including workspace session ids)

## Reconnect button does nothing

- Verify connection details still exist in local storage
- Check authentication method is still valid for the host
